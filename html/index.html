Welcome to Taita Mauche Secondary

Established in 1985, Taita Mauche Secondary School has been a pillar of academic excellence in Njoro, Nakuru County.

Our mission is to provide quality education that nurtures holistic development, fosters critical thinking, and prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century.
