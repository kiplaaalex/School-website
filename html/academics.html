Our Curriculum

Taita Mauche Secondary follows the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) at Junior Secondary and the 8-4-4 system at Senior Secondary.

Our academic program is designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills while meeting all Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD) requirements.
