Admissions Process

Taita Mauche Secondary School admits students into Form 1 (Grade 7) and other classes based on availability.

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds who demonstrate academic potential and commitment to our school values.

Application Submission

Complete the online application form or download and submit a physical form with required documents.

Entrance Assessment

All applicants take an entrance exam in Mathematics, English and General Knowledge.

Interview

Shortlisted candidates and parents attend an interview with the admissions panel.

Admission Offer

Successful candidates receive admission letters with reporting instructions.

Admission Requirements

Form 1 Admission

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • KCPE results slip (original and copy)
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Medical report from recognized hospital
  • Recommendation letter from previous school
  • Transfer letter (for those from other secondary schools)

Other Forms Admission

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Latest academic reports (originals and copies)
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Medical report from recognized hospital
  • Recommendation letter from current school
  • Transfer letter and clearance from previous school
International students must provide equivalency certificates from KNEC and valid study permits.
