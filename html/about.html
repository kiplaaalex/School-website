About Our School

Discover our rich history, mission, and the dedicated team behind Taita Mauche Secondary

Our History

Established in 1985, Taita Mauche Secondary School has been a pillar of academic excellence in Njoro, Nakuru County.

1985

School founded with 50 students and 3 teachers in temporary structures

1992

First permanent classrooms constructed through community fundraising

2005

Recognized as a top-performing school in Nakuru County

2018

New science laboratories and computer center opened

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures holistic development, fosters critical thinking, and prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century through innovative teaching methods and a supportive learning environment.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that produces well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values to contribute positively to society and compete globally in various fields of endeavor.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, ethics, and moral principles in all our actions.

Respect

We value diversity and treat all individuals with dignity, fairness, and compassion.

Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in all aspects of teaching, learning, and school operations.

Teamwork

We collaborate effectively with all stakeholders to achieve our common goals.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and continuous improvement in our teaching methods.

Service

We are committed to serving our community and making a positive impact.
